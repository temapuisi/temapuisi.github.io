14. Rp. 305.450. how to say in sentences?…​


JAWABAN :

Three hundred five thousand four hundred fifty rupiah

PENJELASAN :

305 = Three hundred five (Tiga ratus lima)

'Thousand' aritnya 'Ribu'

450 = Four hundred fifty

Jadi, jika digabung..

Rp. 305.450 = Three hundred five thousand four hundred fifty rupiah

Terjemahan = Tiga ratus lima ribu empat ratus lima puluh rupiah

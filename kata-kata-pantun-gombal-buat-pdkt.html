16. tentukan permutasi dari kata• gombal • trossudh mlem msih aj gombal -,​


Jawaban:

Gombal

Banyak unsur = 6

Unsur sama = 0

Banyak susunan

= 6!

= 6 × 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1

= 30 × 12 × 2

= 360 × 2

= 720 susunan

Tross

Banyak unsur = 5

Unsur sama = 2 (huruf s)

Banyak susunan

= 5! ÷ 2!

= (5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1) ÷ (2 × 1)

= 5 × 4 × 3

= 20 × 3

= 60 susunan

12. 73 1. Yamin and hezzi are camping =(+) Yamin and hezzi are camping (-) (?) ​


Jawaban:

Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Yamin and Hezzi are camping.

(-) Yamin and Hezzi are not camping.

(?) Are Yamin and Hezzi camping?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Present Continuous Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan yang sedang dilakukan dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)

(-) Subejct + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + Subject + Verb-ing + Adverb of time (Now/today)?

Terkadang Adverb of Time juga disebut sebagai Time Signal.

Semoga membantu ya.

24. monolog tentang camping trip


I went on a camping trip last holiday. My mother, father, my two sisters and I went to a camping site that was a 3 hour drive from the city. When we reached the campsite, we searched for an empty part of land to set up our tents. My father went to search for wood to make a camp fire, my mother and I set up the tents while my two sisters were playing around. When night time came, my father lit up the fire and we made s'mores to eat. It was very cold so my sisters both cuddled together to keep themselves warm. My parents and I didn't seem to mind the cold weather. We slept at around midnight. The next day, we went on a hike to the top of the hill. My father led the way, my two sisters following behind, while my mother and I stayed behind. After we reached the top of the hill, we enjoyed the view. After a while, we went back down to the camp site and packed all of our things since it was ready to go home. I like camping and I hope to do it again sometime.

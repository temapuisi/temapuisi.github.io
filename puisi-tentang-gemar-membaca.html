Menyanyi = 15%
Olahraga = 37%
Menari = 18%

Presentase Baca Puisi
100% - (15 + 37 + 18)%
= 100% - 70%
= 30%

Banyak Siswa Gemar Baca Puisi
30/100 x 160
= 3/1 x 16
= 48 siswa160 * 15% = 24 /160 *360 =54° ››menyanyi
160 * 37 % = 59,2 /160 *360 = 133,2 °
160 *18 % = 28,8/160 *360 = 64,8°

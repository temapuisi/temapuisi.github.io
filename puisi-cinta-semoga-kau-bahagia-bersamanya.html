Hope you are happy with your partner.

Answer:


In translating phrase or expression, you need to adjust it with the proper words in the target language as well as its context. Literal or word by word translation isn't recommended as well since it might confuse the meaning in the target language.

The English translation of 'semoga kau bahagia bersama dengan pasanganmu' is

'I hope you're happy with your beloved one.' OR

'I wish that you can be happy with your beloved girlfriend/boy friend' OR

'I wish for your happiness with your beloved one.

26. Apa bahasa inggrisnya : semoga kamu bahagia walaupun tak bersamaku


kelas : SMP
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : love expression
kata kunci : wish, hope

pembahasan:
semoga kamu bahagia walaupun tak bersamaku (saya berharap kamu selalu bahagia meski tanpaku) = I wish you will always be happy without me
atau, I hope you are always happy without me.
atau, Hoping you are always happy without me.

happy di sini berbentuk adjective (kata sifat) dalam kalimat simple future, sehingga be + adjective.
tak bersamaku di sini mirip dengan kata tanpa (bersama)ku = without me.

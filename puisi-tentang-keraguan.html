B: I have an appointment with my best friend tomorrow. We will do a school project together. The project will be submitted on Monday. But my grandma is seriously ill, and my family and I will have to visit her tomorrow.Tell me, what should I do?
A: I guess you should tell your parents that you won't visit your grandma. Your friend need you more than your grandma.
B: I don't know. I'm not sure about it.

A: Fine. Do what you think is the best. I can only give you my opinion.
B: I know. Thank you, A.
A: You're welcome, B.

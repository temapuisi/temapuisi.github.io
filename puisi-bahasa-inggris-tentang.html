Dreams
 live in sleep, (Mimpi hidup dalam tidur,)
Dreams create visions of hopes and fears,  (Mimpi membuat padangan dari harapan dan rasa takut,)
Dreams are more powerful than thoughts,  (Mimpi itu lebih kuat dari pada pemikiran)

Do you dream at all?  (Apakah kamu memimpikannya semua ?)

14. sejarah puisi dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

Poetry as an art form can precede literacy. Many ancient works, from the Indian Vedas (1700-1200 BC) and Zoroaster's Gathas (1,200-900 BC) to Odyssey (800-675 BC), appear to have been composed in poetic form for memorization and oral, in prehistoric and ancient societies. Poetry appears among the earliest records of the most literate culture, with poetic fragments found at the beginning of monoliths, runestones, and stelae.

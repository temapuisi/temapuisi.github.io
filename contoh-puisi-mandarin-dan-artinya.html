Before my bed
There is bright-lit moonlight

疑是地上霜 Yi shi di shang shuang.
So that it seems
Like frost on the ground:

举头望明月 Ju tou wang ming yue,
Lifting my head
I watch the bright moon

低头是故乡 Di tou si gu xiang.
Lowering my head
I dream that I'm home.
